Our Story

Welcome to our brewery, where passion meets craftsmanship in the heart of Punavuori. Our journey began years ago with a shared love for brewing and a vision to create exceptional beers that would bring people together. Today, we are proud to offer a unique taproom experience right here in one of Helsinki’s most vibrant neighborhoods.

The foundation of our brewery was laid by two enthusiastic brewers, Aino Lehikoinen and Eero Erkkilä. With Aino leading the charge as our Head Brewer and Eero by her side as the Assistant Brewer, our team is dedicated to brewing the finest craft beers you’ll ever taste.

Aino Lehikoinen, a seasoned brewer with a penchant for innovation, discovered her love for brewing during her travels through Europe. Inspired by the diverse beer cultures she encountered, Aino returned to Finland with a dream to craft her own beers that would reflect both tradition and creativity. Her dedication to quality and her meticulous attention to detail have earned her a reputation as one of the best brewers in the region.

Our taproom, located in the bustling district of Punavuori, is more than just a place to enjoy great beer. It’s a community hub where locals and visitors alike come together to share stories, make new friends, and celebrate life’s simple pleasures. The warm and welcoming atmosphere of our taproom is the perfect setting to savor our wide selection of craft beers, each brewed with love and care.

At our brewery, we believe that the best beers are those that bring people together. That’s why we host regular events, from beer tasting sessions to live music nights, creating unforgettable experiences for our patrons. We are committed to sustainability and source our ingredients locally whenever possible, ensuring that our beers are not only delicious but also environmentally friendly.

Our story is one of passion, perseverance, and community. It’s a story that continues to evolve with every new brew, every shared pint, and every smiling face that walks through our doors. We invite you to be a part of our journey, to discover the magic of our craft beers, and to make memories that will last a lifetime.

The story of our brewery is deeply intertwined with the rich history and vibrant culture of Punavuori. Known for its eclectic mix of art, music, and culinary delights, Punavuori has always been a place where creativity thrives. It’s this unique atmosphere that inspired us to set up our taproom here, where the community’s spirit of innovation and togetherness mirrors our own values.

Every corner of our taproom tells a story. From the reclaimed wood used in our bar, which once formed the beams of a historic Helsinki building, to the local artists' murals that adorn our walls, we’ve created a space that celebrates our heritage and our commitment to the community. Our taproom isn’t just a place to drink beer; it’s a place to experience a part of Helsinki’s soul.

Aino’s journey to becoming a master brewer began in her childhood, growing up in a small Finnish village where brewing was a cherished tradition. She learned the art of brewing from her grandfather, who crafted beers using age-old recipes passed down through generations. This deep-rooted knowledge, combined with her formal education and experiences abroad, has shaped Aino into the visionary brewer she is today.

Eero, on the other hand, brings a scientific approach to our brewing process. His academic background in chemistry and microbiology, coupled with his hands-on experience in various breweries around the world, has equipped him with the skills to optimize fermentation and create unique, flavorful beers. Together, Aino and Eero form the perfect team, blending tradition with innovation to produce beers that are truly exceptional.

Our brewing philosophy is simple: quality over quantity. We believe that every beer we produce should be a masterpiece, crafted with the finest ingredients and the utmost care. This commitment to excellence is evident in every sip of our beers, whether you’re enjoying a crisp, refreshing lager or a bold, complex IPA.

We’re also passionate about sustainability and making a positive impact on our environment. From using renewable energy in our brewing process to sourcing our ingredients from local farmers, we strive to minimize our carbon footprint and support the community that supports us. We’re proud to be a part of the growing movement towards sustainable brewing and hope to inspire others to join us in this endeavor.

But our story wouldn’t be complete without mentioning the people who make it all possible: our dedicated staff and our loyal patrons. Our team is a close-knit family, each member bringing their own unique skills and passion to the table. Their hard work and enthusiasm are the driving force behind our success, and we’re incredibly grateful for their contributions.

Our patrons, too, play a crucial role in our journey. Their love for our beers, their feedback, and their support have been invaluable. Whether they’re regulars who visit our taproom every week or tourists discovering our beers for the first time, they are the heart and soul of our brewery. We’re honored to have such a wonderful community of beer lovers and look forward to many more years of shared experiences and cherished memories.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our core values of quality, community, and sustainability. We’re excited about the new beers we’ll create, the events we’ll host, and the connections we’ll forge. Our journey is far from over, and we invite you to join us as we continue to grow and evolve.

Thank you for being a part of our story. Here’s to many more years of great beer and good times!

Cheers to great beer, good company, and the joy of brewing!
