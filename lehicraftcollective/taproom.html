Taproom

Our cozy taproom in the heart of Punavuori is the perfect place to unwind and enjoy our handcrafted beers. Here you can find a wide selection of our craft beers, from hoppy IPAs to smooth stouts. We also host regular taproom tastings featuring new releases, seasonal brews, and behind-the-scenes insights into the brewing process. Enjoy our comfortable seating, friendly service, and the vibrant energy of Punavuori.
