Sustainability

At Lehi Craft Collective, we’re more than just a brewery; we’re committed to brewing a better world. As passionate craft beer enthusiasts, we understand the importance of preserving our planet for future generations. That’s why we’re dedicated to brewing responsibly and giving back to the environment we love.

We’re proud to partner with the Finnish Nature Conservation Association to protect Finland’s unique and precious nature. With every purchase of our Baltic Sea IPA, we donate 25% of the proceeds directly to their vital conservation efforts. By supporting their work, we’re helping to safeguard Finland’s forests, lakes, and coastlines for years to come.

But our commitment to sustainability goes beyond our partnership with the Finnish Nature Conservation Association. We’re constantly exploring new ways to reduce our environmental impact. From sourcing local, organic ingredients to minimizing waste and conserving energy, we’re committed to operating in a way that’s both sustainable and socially responsible.

We believe that business can be a force for good. By choosing Lehi Craft Collective, you’re not only enjoying great-tasting craft beer but also supporting a company that is committed to making a positive difference in the world. Join us in our mission to brew a better future.
